# Magia Departamentos — Business Overview

## Context
Magia Departamentos was launched in December 2024 as a major expansion move.
It is a department/beach-articles store located on the corner directly in front of Supermercado Magia (Avenida das Nações, Canasvieiras).

Purpose:
- complement Magia’s commercial ecosystem
- capture tourist demand for leisure/summer/convenience products

## Operating Model vs Supermarket
Unlike the supermarket model (high volume, lower margins), Magia Departamentos is more dependent on:
- higher value-added products
- stronger product presentation
- visual merchandising/layout quality
- better-prepared sales staff
- consultative customer support

Execution quality (team training + management quality) is a major performance lever.

## Product Categories (current)
### Beach equipment
- beach chairs
- umbrellas
- gazebos
- beach carts

### Small appliances
- air fryers
- toasters
- other small home appliances

### Everyday utility products
- thermal bottles (e.g., Stanley-like category)
- towels
- daily-use accessories

## Learning Phase
The operation is still in an early learning cycle (first year):
- product mix calibration
- target customer behavior understanding
- demand pattern learning by season/profile

## Seasonality Profile
Magia Departamentos is highly seasonal, potentially even more than the supermarket.

Observed pattern:
1. Very strong summer sales for store size/team structure.
2. Very weak winter performance, with risk of revenue not covering fixed costs (rent, payroll, utilities).

Current strategic hypothesis:
- operate strongly in summer
- hibernate/close in low season to preserve economics
- reopen for next high season

## Strategic Impact
Magia Departamentos reinforces the same core thesis:
seasonality is a central economic force in Magia businesses.

Implication:
- maximize extraction during summer
- use winter for preparation and next-cycle strengthening rather than forcing low-quality operation.
