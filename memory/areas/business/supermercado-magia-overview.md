# Supermercado Magia — Business Overview

## Context
Supermercado Magia is a food retail business located in Canasvieiras, Florianópolis (Ilha da Magia), in a high-traffic corner on Avenida das Nações.

The store serves two main customer groups:
- local residents
- tourists (especially during summer high season)

## Strategic Location
Magia is positioned in a central flow area near:
- hotels and inns
- restaurants
- tourism-focused commerce

Operational implications:
- strong summer foot traffic
- constant commercial visibility
- high conversion opportunity from tourist movement

## Tourism Dependence (Argentinian Flow)
Canasvieiras has received strong Argentinian tourism flow since the 1990s.
During high season, foreign tourists (especially Argentinians) represent a major share of demand.

Operational effects:
- significant volume spikes in sales
- change in consumption profile
- higher demand for practical/quick-use items
- need for larger temporary teams

## Seasonality as Core Business Driver
Most annual revenue concentration happens between:
- December
- January
- February
- early March

High season requires accelerated operation.
Low season requires:
- strict cost control
- tighter inventory management
- careful financial planning

A strong summer performance strongly influences full-year outcome.

## Company History and Growth
- Founded in 1990
- Started as a small neighborhood market
- Expanded over time with multiple structural reforms and floor/operational area growth
- Current scale: 100+ active employees
- Consolidated as a known local market in Canasvieiras

## Historical Expansion (Lagoa Unit)
Magia previously operated a second unit in Lagoa da Conceição, focused on a higher-income customer profile.
After founder-partner separation, this unit was closed.

## Current Expansion Strategy
In December 2024, Magia expanded into a new format:
- Magia Departamentos (department + beach products store)
- located across from Supermercado Magia, also on Avenida das Nações

## Brand Development
Magia is currently strengthening brand identity and internal culture.
Goal: evolve from a known supermarket to a recognized regional brand with clearer identity.

## Strategic Summary
Supermercado Magia is deeply tied to Canasvieiras tourism dynamics.
Core characteristics:
- high dependence on tourism seasonality
- strategic location advantage
- high-volume summer operation
- gradual expansion into tourism-adjacent business models

Understanding Magia requires understanding local tourism economics and seasonality patterns.
